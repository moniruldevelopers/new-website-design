About JIS College

Welcome to JIS College

The year 2010 marked the beginning of an era as the gates of Jahanara Israil School and College opened to serve southern Bangladesh. Situated in Barishal, we deliver the highest standards of institutional education based on the English National Curriculum.

Our foremost goal is quality modern education at an affordable cost — decentralizing excellence so families don't have to relocate to Dhaka. Today we proudly serve 1,000+ students from Playgroup to College level.
